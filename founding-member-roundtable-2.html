About This Session

The Yorkseed Founding Member Roundtable is an intimate, high-signal virtual gathering for the people building and backing the next generation of venture-grade companies. No panels. No pitches. Just real conversation among a deliberately small, carefully curated group of founders and investors who are in it for the long game.

Each quarter, Founding Members convene to exchange candid perspectives on what's working, where capital is moving, and what the room is seeing that the market hasn't caught up to yet. It's the kind of conversation that doesn't happen in public — and won't, after this.

This is the Q3 session — now closed to Founding Members only. The open preview is over. What started as a one-time window is now a quarterly ritual reserved for those who committed to the inner circle.
