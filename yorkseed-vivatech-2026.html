Questions

Everything you need to know before applying.

Is this really free?↓
Yes — completely free. Yorkseed and START Paris cover the cost of your VivaTech pass. You only need to cover your own travel and accommodation to Paris.
Who is this for?↓
Built for ambitious students and recent graduates — whether you're already building a startup, exploring ideas, or simply want to be around the best founders and investors in Europe. You don't need to be a founder to apply.
Do I need to cover travel and accommodation?↓
Yes — the free pass covers your VivaTech entry. Travel and accommodation are your responsibility. The delegate toolkit includes an accommodation guide with options near Porte de Versailles at every price range.
When will I receive my ticket?↓
The START Paris team will email your official VivaTech pass in May–June 2026, ahead of the event. Once you submit the form, no further action is needed.
What are the dates?↓
VivaTech 2026 runs June 17–20 at Paris Expo Porte de Versailles. The application deadline is June 15.
What if I can't attend all 4 days?↓
That's fine — even a single day at VivaTech is worth the trip. Your pass is valid for all four days, but you're not required to attend all of them.
What Yorkseed side events are included?↓
Delegation members get access to Yorkseed's curated Paris Tech Week side events — dinners, workshops, founder meetups, and cross-border gatherings. Details are shared through the delegate toolkit and WhatsApp group after you apply.
