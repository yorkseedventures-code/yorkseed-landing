Ugly Talk:
The Future of Fashion & Beauty Tech

📅 Wednesday, June 3, 2026 · 7:00 PM ET
📍 Belong NYC · Manhattan
🎟 Open registration
What's the Ugly Truth

Beauty tech is booming, but growth has made the category louder, not clearer. AI diagnostics, AR try-ons, personalization, and smart devices are pulling more founders into the space — but more innovation also means more hype, more copycats, and more brands fighting for the same customer attention.

For founders, a strong product is just the entry fee. The real edge comes from knowing what actually drives trust, repeat purchase, and loyalty in a market where branding and novelty can fake momentum. This room is for people who want to separate signal from noise and figure out which trends are truly shaping the future of beauty tech.

Taking the Stage
ZS
Zehra Soysal
Founder & CEO · Savanah.ai
Zehra's career began at Google in business strategy, followed by impactful roles at BCG across Europe, the Middle East, and Asia. She played a key role in establishing a tech joint venture with Turkey's largest conglomerate. After earning her MBA from Harvard Business School in 2022, she joined Metaphysic.ai in New York, helping scale operations through its $1.4B acquisition. She is now the founder and CEO of Savanah.ai — a visual AI platform helping brands create e-commerce imagery at scale and optimize conversion.

More speakers coming soon

What We Don't Sugarcoat
In an industry full of noise and competition, how do you separate real signal from hype?
How do you fight copycats in a market where products move fast and differentiation can disappear overnight?
What trends actually reflect customer demand — and what are customers burning for now that they weren't asking for before?
How does AI actually matter here — from diagnostics and personalization to discovery, conversion, and retention?
