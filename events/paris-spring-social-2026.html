About This Event

An intimate evening bringing together founders, investors, operators, and ecosystem leaders for meaningful conversations and high-signal connections in Paris.

Hosted by Yorkseed, this curated gathering is designed for people actively building, investing, and shaping the future across technology, venture, and innovation. Expect a relaxed yet refined atmosphere focused on genuine relationship building rather than transactional networking.

Whether you are raising capital, deploying capital, scaling globally, or looking to connect with like-minded people within the venture ecosystem — this evening is designed to foster thoughtful conversations and long-term connections.

Due to the intimate nature of the gathering, capacity is limited and attendance is subject to approval.

The right rooms. The right people. The right tools — for every step of the journey, including the ones you have not thought about yet.
