Venture Vitality:
HealthTech Investor Roundtable

💻 Virtual · Zoom
🔒 Off the record · 30 select investors
🎟 Accredited investors only
About This Session

An off-the-record virtual tactical roundtable for accredited investors shaping the future of HealthTech. Whether you're deploying capital in Digital Health, BioTech, MedTech, or FemTech, this session is designed to give you clarity, strategy, and peer insight — beyond the pitch decks.

In a market clouded by shrinking exits and capital uncertainty, this is a space for accredited investors to speak candidly about what's working, what's not, and what's coming next.

No startup pitches. Just open collaboration. Hosted by PulsePoint Path, Yorkseed, and Health Board Advisors, this high-trust gathering brings together 30 select investors for a private, actionable conversation on deal flow, fund performance, follow-on strategies, and the real markers of startup success.

What You'll Gain
🧠Investor Clarity
Raw insights on what's truly scaling in HealthTech today
Strategy swaps and deal feedback from seasoned peers
A pitch-free environment to challenge assumptions
🎯Network Alignment
High-trust relationships with fellow LPs, angels, and fund managers
Frameworks and co-investment opportunities
Benchmarking against current market dynamics
💬Visibility
Featured judge opportunity at PulsePoint Path's HealthTech Showdown
Spotlighted on the Funding Her Future podcast series
Who This Is For
Accredited investors deploying in Digital Health, BioTech, MedTech, or FemTech
LPs, angels, and fund managers actively sourcing or co-investing
Strategic advisors and operators with capital market exposure in healthcare
Corporate venture and family office investors with health sector focus
Featured Host
Sabrina Runbeck, MPH, MHS, PA-C
Co-Founder & CEO · PulsePoint Path
A dynamic healthcare strategist with 24 years of experience in clinical medicine, public health, executive coaching, and strategic consulting. Having navigated burnout across 11 major healthcare systems, Sabrina now empowers healthcare founders to optimise their business models, enhance visibility, and drive lasting social impact. Host of the top 5% globally ranked Provider's Edge podcast. TEDx speaker featured on FOX, CBS, and ABC.
