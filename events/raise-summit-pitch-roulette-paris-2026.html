Paris Ecosystem Mixer
RAISE Summit Pitch Roulette

📅 Monday, July 6, 2026 · 6:00–8:30 PM
📍 Near Carrousel du Louvre · Paris
🎤 Pitch Roulette · 2 min, 1 shot
About This Event

As RAISE Summit brings together Europe's top founders and investors, Yorkseed invites you to an intimate ecosystem mixer just steps from the Carrousel du Louvre — a private indoor terrace on the 2nd floor of a restaurant bar.

Join an early-evening gathering of curated global builders: founders scaling bold ideas, investors backing long-term vision, and ecosystem allies bridging global corridors.

Set in a charming venue near the summit action, this mixer offers a chance to slow down, connect with intention, and toast to the serendipitous collisions that make this ecosystem thrive.

Whether you're a Paris-based innovator or visiting for the summit, come meet the humans behind the headlines over happy hour, warm conversation, and what's-next energy.

🎁 RAISE Summit Perks: Everyone who attends gets a free month of Pro from Windsurf, plus 25% off GenAI from genai.works for event attendees.

🎤 Pitch Roulette — NYC Style

Since our founder hails from NYC, we're bringing some Big Apple energy to the room — and giving you the mic. We're opening up 3 quickfire pitch rounds where anyone can introduce themselves. First come, first serve — step up and shine!

⏱ 6:30–6:40 PM ⏱ 7:15–7:25 PM ⏱ 8:00–8:10 PM
"Hi, I'm [Name], founder of [Company], based in [City]. I'm looking for [X], and I can help with [Y]. Find me after!"

Short, clear, and memorable. You've got 25 seconds — make it count. Already pitched? Feel free to go again in a later slot.

📱 Bonus tip: conference QR codes are nice, but your LinkedIn or WhatsApp QR is 🔥. Screenshot it and be ready to connect fast.
