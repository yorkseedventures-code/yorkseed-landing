About This Event

A curated fintech and AI gathering during New York Tech Week, bringing together founders and investors operating at the forefront of financial innovation.

This is an application-based event designed for those actively building, raising, and deploying capital across fintech, AI, payments, wealthtech, and regtech.

The evening features a curated lineup of startup pitches from selected founders, followed by an investor panel sharing real perspectives on what is getting funded in today's market. Conversations continue through structured and open networking designed to create meaningful connections and tangible outcomes.

Founders interested in pitching will be reviewed in advance, with selected participants moving through a structured evaluation process. The room is intentionally sized to maintain quality, access, and strong signal across attendees.

This is not a volume-driven event. It is built for focused conversations, real alignment, and high-context networking.

Featured Speaker
Bhuva Shakti
Founder & CEO · Wallet Max
Bhuva Shakti (she/her) is the futuristic founder and CEO driving sustainable innovation at Wallet Max, a global community of corporate executives, policy leaders, and venture capital investors expanding fundraising access for high-growth impact startups. With an MBA from Columbia University and three decades as a senior executive director on Wall Street, Bhuva has managed diverse financial portfolios and launched digital transformation products for investment banks, capital markets, and payment networks. Her leadership and risk governance oversight during the global financial crisis and the pandemic was pivotal for regulatory compliance and M&A at the world's top banks and credit rating institutions.

Bhuva is a board director and C-Suite advisor for people and planet friendly businesses, and a keynote and TEDx speaker advancing economic inclusion and climate-fintech partnerships. She is the USA Country Director for World Business Angels Investment Forum, Chief Ethics & Culture Officer for Women in AI worldwide, and founder of Bhuva's Impact Global — offering board and enterprise risk advisory services for public and private corporations.
