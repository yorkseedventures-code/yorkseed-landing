About This Event

During NY Tech Week, In Pulse and Yorkseed are hosting a highly curated Venture Salon in Manhattan. We are bringing together only 75 founders and investors for an invite-only, closed-door evening designed for people who are already building, deploying, and scaling.

If you've been in the right rooms before, you know the difference.
