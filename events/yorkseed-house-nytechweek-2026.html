About Yorkseed House

Yorkseed House returns during NY Tech Week / Latino Tech Week with a curated multi-day pop-up experience hosted inside the Belong NYC space in Lower Manhattan's SoHo.

As the official venue partner, Belong NYC is opening its doors to founders, investors, operators, creators, and ecosystem leaders for a week of intentional programming, curated gatherings, venture conversations, and community-driven experiences during one of the most important weeks in tech.

More than just a venue — Yorkseed House transforms the Belong NYC space into a central gathering point for the global startup and venture ecosystem throughout NY Tech Week. Designed as a hybrid between a venture lounge, hospitality hub, community clubhouse, and founder meeting space, it creates an environment where meaningful collisions happen naturally.

The right rooms. The right people. The right tools — for every step of the journey, including the ones you have not thought about yet.
