About This Event

Yorkseed's first-ever gathering in Bali — a curated sunset evening during DCD Connect APAC, bringing together founders, investors, family offices, infrastructure operators, and ecosystem leaders shaping the future of AI, data centers, energy, and digital infrastructure.

As Yorkseed continues to expand globally, this evening marks the beginning of our presence in Southeast Asia's fastest-growing venture and infrastructure ecosystem.

No panels. No presentations. No formal agenda. Just a curated evening of conversations with ambitious people building and investing across the ecosystem.

If you're attending DCD Connect APAC, or know someone who will be in Bali for the conference, we'd love to have you join us.

Sometimes the most valuable opportunities come from the conversations that happen after the conference ends.

The right rooms. The right people. The right tools — for every step of the journey, including the ones you have not thought about yet.
