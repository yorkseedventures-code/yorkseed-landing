About This Event

A highly curated, intimate evening during Milan AI Tech Week, bringing together a select group of founders, investors, operators, and creatives shaping the future of technology and innovation.

Hosted by Yorkseed and EdgeCraft Group, this aperitivo is designed for meaningful conversations, thoughtful connections, and high-signal interactions in a relaxed yet refined setting in the heart of Milan.

Expect an intentional room where venture meets conversation — whether you are actively raising, deploying capital, expanding globally, or simply looking to connect with like-minded people building the future.

This gathering is part of the Yorkseed global series, connecting founders and investors through curated experiences across major innovation hubs around the world.

Due to the intimate nature of the venue, capacity is extremely limited. Approved guests will receive final confirmation.
