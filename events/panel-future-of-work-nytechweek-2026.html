Panel: Future of Work

📅 Thursday, June 4, 2026 · 3:30–6:00 PM EDT
📍 New York, NY
👔 Business Casual
About This Event

Whether you are a founder or a corporate leader, the future of work is a race to redefine the human element through scalable technology. Success begins at the foundation, where high-agency builders look beyond simple automation to architect environments that turn baseline people operations into mission-critical infrastructure.

Join Founders Foundry for an independent, flagship panel bringing together three distinct lenses — an organizational scientist, an enterprise operator, and a venture investor — to map out a raw blueprint for team growth and organizational resilience from deal flow to scale.

We are defining a new archetype for the modern builder: the polymath creator. The core focus is cognitive augmentation over headcount replacement — leveraging AI as a tool to expand human agency rather than substitute it.

In an ambiguous world, elite leaders perceive technological uncertainty as an opportunity rather than a fear, empowering teams to navigate with what they have. Come prepared with your sharpest questions for an organic, provocative conversation featuring real-time crowd polling to tackle the actual bottlenecks choking your organization today.
