The Salon

During RAISE Summit, beyond the conference halls, Valois convened a carefully curated gathering of the people shaping the future of private capital.

Hosted in an intimate residence overlooking the Louvre, the evening drew inspiration from the great Parisian salons — bringing together senior allocators and investment leaders for thoughtful conversation in an environment built on discretion, trust, and long-term relationships.

There was no stage. Just meaningful conversation among peers responsible for deploying capital, stewarding institutions, and shaping investment across global markets.
