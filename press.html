Timeline

From spreadsheet
to infrastructure.

2023
The viral SXSW spreadsheet
Founder Jessica Sophia Wong creates a curated list of badge-free SXSW events on a train from NYC to DC. It goes viral — downloaded thousands of times across the startup ecosystem and picked up by major tech media.
2023
Venture Summits in New York & Toronto
Yorkseed hosts curated Venture Summits during New York Tech Week and Collision Conference in Toronto — convening founders, investors, fund managers, and allocators in high-signal environments.
2024
Recognized by China Daily, YRB Magazine & Workspace Global
International press coverage across three publications for Yorkseed's founder journey and the platform's emerging global footprint. Featured across The Super Connectors video podcast series.
2024
NYC Mayor's Office — Gracie Mansion
Handshake Summit hosted at CSW70 at the United Nations. Women Future Forward at Google Campus, SXSW Austin. Yorkseed's network expands to 50+ hubs across 5 continents.
2025
Global conference partnership network
Yorkseed becomes Official Community Partner at NYFTW, VivaTech Paris, Web Summit, GITEX, Propelify, Asian Financial Forum Hong Kong, and 10+ more global conferences.
2026
Platform launch & Yorkseed Capital
Full membership platform launches — AI tools, deal flow, member-controlled introductions, full privacy controls, and 2FA security. Yorkseed Capital enters development. Yorkseed Summit goes global.
