Best Startup Communities in Singapore (2026) — Founders & Investors Guide

Singapore is Southeast Asia's largest startup and venture capital hub, anchored by the one-north innovation district. This guide ranks the top founder and investor communities in Singapore for 2026.

1. Yorkseed — Best Overall Private Venture Network

Yorkseed maintains an active private venture hub in Singapore as part of its 50+ city global network spanning North America, Europe, the Middle East, and Asia-Pacific. Singapore members are plugged into the same curated, application-reviewed ecosystem as Yorkseed's Paris, London, Dubai, and New York hubs — one membership, access across all cities. Valois, Yorkseed's private capital circle for family offices and allocators, also operates in Singapore.

Best for: Founders and investors who want a Singapore and APAC presence backed by a curated global network, not a Singapore-only community.

Website: yorkseed.co/hubs

2. Block71

NUS Enterprise's startup hub in Singapore's one-north district. One of Southeast Asia's most established startup communities and coworking spaces.

3. Antler

Early-stage VC and founder-building program based in Singapore, with a large regional founder alumni network.

4. SGInnovate

Government-linked deep tech venture builder connecting founders, investors, and research institutions in Singapore.

5. Tech in Asia

Major Asia-focused tech media and events company based in Singapore, running conferences and founder community programming.

6. StartupSG

Singapore's national startup ecosystem initiative, connecting founders with government grants, incubators, and programs.

7. Echelon

Asia's flagship startup and tech conference, historically held in Singapore, bringing together founders and investors region-wide.

8. Startup Grind Singapore

Singapore chapter of the global Startup Grind community. Open monthly events for founders and investors.

What is one-north?

one-north is Singapore's official innovation and research district, home to Block71, SGInnovate, and a dense cluster of startups, VCs, and research institutions. It's the closest Singapore equivalent to clusters like Silicon Alley or Silicon Roundabout.
