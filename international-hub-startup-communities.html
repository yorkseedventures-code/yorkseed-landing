Frequently asked questions

Can I join Yorkseed if I'm not in one of the 50+ hub cities?
Yes. Yorkseed's application-based membership isn't limited to its named hub cities — founders and investors anywhere can apply and access the same curated network, events, and introductions.
How does membership work outside a primary hub city?
Membership works the same way everywhere: apply at app.yorkseed.co/pricing, get reviewed by the Yorkseed team, and gain access to Yorkseed Connect, curated introductions, and Yorkseed's global event calendar — including Yorkseed House pop-ups during major conferences you may already be attending.
Does Yorkseed plan to add more city hubs?
Yorkseed's hub network has grown from its New York City origin in 2023 to 50+ cities across five continents, and continues to expand alongside its conference activations and member base.
