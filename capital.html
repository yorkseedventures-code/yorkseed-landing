Yorkseed
Capital.

Selective, deal-by-deal co-investments structured as SPVs. Access to early-stage and growth companies sourced directly from the Yorkseed network. Offered privately to qualified investors — not publicly solicited.

Request private access → Send an inquiry
Access is by application · Qualified investors only · Not a public offering
What it is

Co-investment access
built on trust.

Yorkseed Capital structures selective SPVs — Special Purpose Vehicles — to provide curated, deal-by-deal access to early-stage and growth companies. Every opportunity is sourced through trusted relationships within the Yorkseed ecosystem.

This is not a fund. There is no blind pool. You evaluate each deal individually — and decide whether to participate. Full information on every opportunity before any commitment.

Opportunities are offered privately to qualified investors within our network and are not publicly solicited. Access is by application and subject to review.

Deal-by-deal — no blind pool
Every opportunity is evaluated individually. You see the full deal, decide to participate or not, on your own timeline.
SPV structure
Each investment is structured as a Special Purpose Vehicle — clean, simple, purpose-built for one deal.
Network-sourced deal flow
Every company comes through the Yorkseed ecosystem — vetted founders, active builders, trusted relationships. Not cold sourced.
Qualified investors only
Access is restricted to qualified and accredited investors. Applications are reviewed before any opportunity is shared.
Private — not publicly solicited
Opportunities are shared privately within the network. Nothing is advertised publicly or distributed broadly.
How it works

Simple. Selective.
Deal by deal.

01
Apply for access
Submit an application through the Yorkseed Investor Circle. Applications are reviewed — not all are accepted.
02
Receive deal memos
Approved investors receive private deal memos when new opportunities are available. Full information before any decision.
03
Decide on each deal
You evaluate each opportunity independently. Participate in the ones that fit your thesis — pass on the ones that don't.
04
Invest via SPV
Each investment is structured as a clean SPV. Simple documentation, straightforward process, one deal at a time.
Who it's for

Built for serious
capital allocators.

Yorkseed Capital is for qualified investors who want curated, relationship-sourced deal flow — without committing to a blind fund.

👼
Angel investors
Accredited angels who want curated early-stage deal flow sourced through trusted relationships — not cold inbound.
🏛️
Family offices
Family offices seeking selective co-investment access alongside trusted co-investors in the Yorkseed network.
💼
Venture investors
VC investors and fund managers looking for co-investment opportunities outside their core fund mandate.
🏢
Corporate venture
CVCs and corporate innovation arms seeking strategic co-investment in early-stage companies within the Yorkseed ecosystem.
