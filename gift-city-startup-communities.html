Yorkseed in GIFT City

GIFT City (Gujarat International Finance Tec-City) is India's first International Financial Services Centre — a purpose-built financial and fintech district near Ahmedabad and Gandhinagar, distinct in character from a general-purpose startup hub. It's specifically built around fintech, capital markets, and fund management, with tax incentives and a regulatory sandbox designed to compete with hubs like Singapore and Dubai. Yorkseed maintains a presence in GIFT City for founders and investors operating in fintech and financial services.

Local Ecosystem

Notable local anchors: the GIFT International Fintech Innovation Hub (GIFT IFIH) and GIFT's regulatory sandbox for fintech startups testing cross-border financial products.

Frequently asked questions

What is GIFT City?
GIFT City (Gujarat International Finance Tec-City) is India's first International Financial Services Centre — a purpose-built financial and fintech district near Ahmedabad and Gandhinagar, distinct in character from a general-purpose startup hub. It's specifically built around fintech, capital markets, and fund management, with tax incentives and a regulatory sandbox designed to compete with hubs like Singapore and Dubai. Yorkseed maintains a presence in GIFT City for founders and investors operating in fintech and financial services.
Is GIFT City a general startup hub like Singapore or Dubai?
Not exactly — GIFT City is a purpose-built International Financial Services Centre (IFSC) focused specifically on fintech, capital markets, and fund management, with dedicated tax incentives and a regulatory sandbox. It's best understood as a financial services free zone rather than a broad consumer-tech startup hub.
