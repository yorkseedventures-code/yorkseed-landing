Why we built this
We kept watching people
look for capital, connections,
and resources in a circle
that only went one way.

Most platforms are built for one side of the table. Founders only. Investors only. We built Yorkseed after watching the entire global venture ecosystem up close — what was working, what was broken, and what was missing for every player in it.

Serious founders struggle to raise — not because they aren't ready, but because their local ecosystem has no relevant capital, and the signal they're trying to send gets buried in noise. In a world where warm introductions are everything, cold outreach rarely lands. The right investor is out there. Getting to them is the problem.

On the other side, investors face the same wall from the other direction. Quality deal flow is hard to find when you're drowning in pitches that don't fit your thesis, your stage, or your sector. The great founders exist. Finding them — reliably, efficiently, across markets — is what's broken.

And the networks that were supposed to solve this? Built for one side. Local by default. Passive in design. A database of people hoping someone else makes the move.

Yorkseed is the answer to both sides at once — the first multi-directional platform where founders get to the right investors, investors find quality deal flow, and every other player in the ecosystem has infrastructure built specifically for their role. Not everyone gets in. Membership is approval-based, reviewed by our team, and re-verified every year — because the integrity of this network is the product.

How it works

From application
to aligned capital

Yorkseed is not a marketplace you scroll. It is curated infrastructure with intention at every step.

01

Request access

Applications are reviewed to preserve signal quality. Tell us your role, what you're building or investing in, and what you need from the network.

02

Build your profile

Stage, sector, thesis, geography, check size — your profile powers the matching engine. Choose exactly what to show and who can see it. Full privacy controls and 2FA security from day one.

03

Connect on your terms

Send intro requests to members you want to meet. The other party must accept before any conversation opens. You control who can reach you — and who you're visible to — at all times.

04

Use the tools

Run your raise, evaluate deals, source LP capital, score an investor, decode a term sheet — 15 purpose-built tools inside one environment — including free SAFE & convertible note calculators.

05

Show up globally

Join curated gatherings at SXSW, Web Summit, Davos, VivaTech, GITEX, and 25+ other conferences. Yorkseed activates where the ecosystem moves.

06

Relationships compound

Every introduction, every tool run, every event — deepens your position in the global ecosystem. Access and trust build over time, not overnight.
