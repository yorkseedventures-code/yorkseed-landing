Why we built this
We kept watching people look for capital, connections, and resources in a circle that only went one way.

Most platforms are built for one side of the table. Founders only. Investors only. We built Yorkseed after watching the entire global venture ecosystem up close — what was working, what was broken, and what was missing for every player in it.

Serious founders struggle to raise — not because they aren't ready, but because their local ecosystem has no relevant capital, and the signal they're trying to send gets buried in noise. In a world where warm introductions are everything, cold outreach rarely lands. The right investor is out there. Getting to them is the problem.

On the other side, investors face the same wall from the other direction. Quality deal flow is hard to find when you're drowning in pitches that don't fit your thesis, your stage, or your sector. The great founders exist. Finding them — reliably, efficiently, across markets — is what's broken.

And the networks that were supposed to solve this? Built for one side. Local by default. Passive in design. A database of people hoping someone else makes the move.

Yorkseed is the answer to both sides at once — the first multi-directional platform where founders get to the right investors, investors find quality deal flow, and every other player in the ecosystem has infrastructure built specifically for their role.

Jessica Sophia Wong
Founder & CEO · Yorkseed · est. 2023
Not everyone gets in. Membership is approval-based, reviewed by our team, and re-verified every year — because the integrity of this network is the product.
The platform

Not a database.
Relationship infrastructure.

Most networking platforms are passive — a directory of profiles waiting for someone to make the first move. Yorkseed Connect is different. It is active infrastructure designed around how relationships in venture actually work.

Every connection on Connect is mutual. Intro requests require both parties to agree before a conversation opens. Your visibility is yours to control at every stage — you choose what you show, who can see it, and when.

Underneath the relationships sit 15+ purpose-built intelligence tools for pitch, diligence, fundraising, and capital structuring. Not generic AI. Not a chatbot. Tools designed for specific jobs in the venture lifecycle.

Connect is the platform layer underneath the broader Yorkseed ecosystem — the conferences, the city hubs, the community. Membership gives you access to all of it.

The room within the room.

Valois is the private upper layer of Yorkseed — an invitation-only network for family offices, HNWIs, endowments, sovereign wealth funds, and GP/LPs operating at the intersection of capital, access, and trust.

It is not a platform. Not a club. Not a perk bundle. It is private relationship infrastructure — a Circle of people who move capital, broker relationships, and make decisions in rooms most people do not reach.
