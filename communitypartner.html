Partnership FAQ

Common questions
answered.

Who should submit a partnership inquiry? ↓
Conference organizers, institutional ecosystem platforms, venture communities, investor and founder networks, accelerator programs, government-backed initiatives, and aligned sponsors seeking structured collaboration with Yorkseed.
Does Yorkseed publish partnership pricing publicly? ↓
No. Partnership frameworks are discussed upon inquiry based on scope, integration level, and strategic alignment.
How are partnerships evaluated? ↓
Yorkseed evaluates collaborations based on audience composition, investor and founder density, geographic relevance, activation scope, long-term strategic value, and degree of ecosystem integration.
Are all partnership requests accepted? ↓
No. Yorkseed maintains a selective partnership framework to preserve signal quality and capital alignment across its global activations. We grow intentionally.
What collaboration models exist? ↓
Partnerships may be structured (defined scope and integration) or reciprocal (clear strategic value exchange). Select collaborations may include formal sponsorship or institutional partnership frameworks discussed upon inquiry.
Can virtual events be shared within the Yorkseed network? ↓
Virtual events may only be distributed within Yorkseed's network if formally affiliated through structured partnership, sponsorship, or official co-hosting with visible Yorkseed branding. Digital distribution is reserved for aligned partners to maintain signal quality.
